Step 4 ^top^

Now lets add some content to your page. This page is going to serve as your hompage for Infinity Pi club and able to be accessed from our website. Create headers and add some text to your paragraph to say a little about you and what your page is for. We will add some more later so dont worry about adding to much.


Step 5 ^top^

Now double click on your hmtl file, while leaving the notpad file open. The file should open up in your web browser, because you saved it with the .htm or .html extention (an extention is the ending of the file name, like myresume.DOCX or funnycatmeme.GIF). See how it just looks like a word document sort of. plain, white, flush left (all lined up on the left side.), lets change that.

Open a new notepad file (by right clicking on the notepad icon and then left clicking on the notepad in that menu that pops up.) and save it as insert your name_infinity_homepage_stylesheet.css.

Next go back to your html hompage file in notepad and add:

    link rel="stylesheet" href="insert your name_infinity_homepage_stylesheet.css."

inside your head tags. *dont forget your opening and closings < > and < / >

Step 7 ^top^

Next we are going to add some pictures. Read this link to see how to add images to your page.
  • HTML Images
When adding images keep in mind that some images that you may find on the internet cannot be used because of copywrite issues. When choosing images that are not yours be sure to follow these steps.
  1. go to google images and search for the image you would like, like dog or trees
  2. when your images appear click on tools at the top of the page
  3. under your drop down options that appear choose usage rights
  4. under usage rights select labeled for noncommercial reuse with modifications
  5. from this point you can use any images you see that are still in your search results.
Add an image to the main section of your webpage. **I HIGHLY suggest that you DO NOT add an image of yourself, your friends, or your family.**

Step 8 ^top^

Now if you want to embed a website or part of a website like a youtube video or a google map you will need to use something called an iframe. To learn more about iframes read this link.
  • HTML iframes
Most sites like youtube and google maps will have an option to share and one way to share is with an embedded link. all you have to do is copy and paste that embed link they give you into the main body of your webpage. For this site you do not have to create your own iframe links, but somewhere on your site include an iframe from an outside site. (I like EMBEDING giphy.com gifs)
