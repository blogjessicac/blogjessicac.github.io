Congratulations on finishing a Dev Plan! Those are hard. Just know what you came up with can be changed and updated as you go along.

Remember a Development Plan is a TOOL not a RULE.





Now that you have a clear idea of what you want to create let's get started. Make a webpage that looks like the one below and fill in all the blanks. Connect that page to your Dev Page (you can use secret or non-secret links)

We also want you to make a "message" page (see the bullet point below) and connect that page to your homepage (use a non-secret link).



  • Goal:
    • The smallest part of my dream project that will give me a sense of accompolishment is _________________.
  • Message:
    • I will create a web page connected to my homepage featuring this small part of my project. The page I create will sum up my Dream Project in full. The page will also give viewers a taste of what I plan to create. The link to that page is ___________________.
  • Feed-Back:
    • Once the page is complete, I will email the link to Infinity Pi's Email. They will distribute the link to my fellow club members so they can give me feedback. The summary of what they said is _________________________.
  • Milestones:
    • My first milestone is four weeks from today, so ________. On Milestone day, I will reflect on what I have done and what, if anything, I have left to do. The summary of that reflection is ____________________.
  • Evaluation:
    • At the beginning of this first strike what did I envision its success looking like?____________________. At the end of this first strike, what does my project actually look like?_____________________. How will the difference or similarity between the two inform what I do next? __________________.
