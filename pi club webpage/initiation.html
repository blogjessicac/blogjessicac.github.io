Using only W3Schools.Com HTML and CSS tutorials, create a Homepage (website) that we can add to our Pi Homepage. Your Homepage must contain the following:

  • At least TWO HEADERS of different sizes
  • A minimum of ONE < p > < /p >
  • Your choice of an ORDERED LIST, UNORDERED LIST, OR TABLE
  • A minimum of ONE PICTURE (whose size has been adjusted with code)
  • PADDING inside the Body
  • A COLORED BACKGROUND or COLORED TEXT (you can have some colored text and some non-colored text or multi-colored text)
  • Must have A LINK back to the Infinity Pi Club's Homepage
  • Must have an EXTERNAL CSS

One you have finished your page bring your files to the next club meeting. If you have any questions bring those to the next club meeting instead.
